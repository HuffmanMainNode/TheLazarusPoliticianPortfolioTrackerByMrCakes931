# The Lazarus Politician Portfolio Tracker: A Constitutional Mandate for Transparency

## 🦅 The First Amendment Directive
*"Congress shall make no law respecting an establishment of religion, or prohibiting the free exercise thereof; or abridging the freedom of speech, or of the press; or the right of the people peaceably to assemble, and to petition the Government for a redress of grievances."*

This repository is an exercise of our constitutional right to petition the government for a redress of grievances. We assert that true democratic representation is impossible without absolute financial transparency. The people have a fundamental, unalienable right to oversee the financial actions, portfolios, and wealth accumulation of their elected officials.

## ⚖️ Demand for Accountability (1980 - Present)
For decades, the lines between public service and private enrichment have been blurred. We demand retroactive and real-time accountability for all financial actions, trades, property acquisitions, and corporate affiliations of public officials from **1980 to the present day**.

This tracker is not just a tool; it is a permanent, immutable record designed to expose insider trading, flag conflicts of interest, and map the flow of dark money that undermines our republic.

## 🚨 CALL TO ACTION: The Open-Source OSINT Initiative
We are calling on all citizens, programmers, data scientists, investigative journalists, and cyber groups. The scale of systemic financial obfuscation requires a decentralized, massive response.

**We need you to:**
*   **Crowdsource OSINT:** Investigate shell companies, trace family member stock trades, and uncover offshore accounts linked to public officials.
*   **Contribute Data:** Push verifiable financial disclosures, SEC filings, and property records to this repository.
*   **Develop Algorithms:** Enhance our `RedFlag_Engine` and `Ranking_Engine` with advanced heuristic models to automatically detect market manipulation and bribery risks.
*   **Host Nodes:** Help decentralize the ingestion engines and database to ensure this information remains uncensorable and permanently available.
*   **Expand the Tracker:** Integrate new data sources, prediction markets, and global news sentiment analysis to predict and expose corrupt trades before they happen.

This is a decentralized audit of the United States Government. Join the pursuit.
